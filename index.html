ABOUT US

More Than an NGO - We Are a Movement

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). Based in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, we operate across India addressing critical societal issues through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

Our mission is to serve communities compassionately and our vision is to create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow with dignity and respect.

  • ✔ Section 8 Registered - Licensed by Central Government
  • ✔ 80G & 12A Certified - Tax-exempt for donors
  • ✔ CSR-1 Registered - Corporate Partnership Eligible
  • ✔ NITI Aayog Registered
  • ✔ IAF 150 9001:2015 Certified
